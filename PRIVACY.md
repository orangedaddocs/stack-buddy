# Privacy

Stack Buddy is designed to be local-first.

There is no account system.

There is no database.

There is no cloud sync.

Scenarios are stored as JSON files on your own machine.

Exports are stored as local files.

Saved chats, if used, are stored as local Markdown files.

The deterministic calculator can be used without AI.

If you use optional AI features, your scenario may be sent to the AI provider or endpoint you configure.

Your privacy then depends on that provider or endpoint.

For maximum privacy:

- Run Stack Buddy locally.
- Do not enable AI features.
- Or connect it to a local model or private endpoint you trust.
- Do not paste seed phrases, wallet information, tax documents, or private financial records into chat.

Stack Buddy should never request, store, or process your seed phrase.
